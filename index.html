FAQs

The installer gets blocked by my antivirus?

The tool was built using ElectronJS and is free of any malware, viruses or other malicious software! You can check out the most recent VirusTotal scan result here. If Windows warns you, it's because I can't afford a rather expensive code signing certificate to validate the installer - Just click "More info" and "Run anyway" to proceed with the installation.

How can I get rid of the purple screens?

There currently is no easy way to get rid of them. Twitch is responsible for the embeded player and the only possibilities are either subscribing to the channel you are watching or watching a streamer that is neither affiliate nor partner.

Can I give you feedback or feature requests?

Sure! You are very welcome to report bugs, give feedback and suggest features for the Stream Moderation Tool here.

Am I eligible for Twitch drops?

Unfortunately as of now you seem to not be eligible for Twitch drops by watching with the Stream Moderation Tool alone. You can bypass this by opening the stream a second time in a muted browser tab and have it play in the background though.

I am having issues loading?

If you are stuck on loading try to restart the tool and everything should be back to normal. This might also happen if Twitch is experiencing high loads or if you are running too many chats on a rather slow machine. In these cases just wait a bit longer or restart.

Will there be frequent updates?

I will do my best to frequently update the tool and keep things running. There will also be improvements and feature additions, while always keeping track of changes on Twitch's side.

Can I change the language?

Yes, the tool currently comes in English and German, while hopefully being translated in more languages soon. If you would like to help translate the tool in your language, please feel free to contact me.
